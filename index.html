    About TechMaster

    Everyone deserves a big package

    TechMaster is a dedicated community for aspiring tech professionals and seasoned engineers to engage in hands-on interview preparation. By conducting mock interviews, peer reviews, and gamified point distributions, we foster an environment of continuous learning and growth. Participate, earn points, increase your CTC potential, and become an industry-ready engineering master!
